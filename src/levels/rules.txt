-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Collect all gems to unlock the exit
• Avoid zombies — each hit costs you a life
• Every collectible has a 15% chance to activate FREEZE:
    - All zombies stop moving for 3 seconds
• Every collectible has a 15% chance to activate DOUBLE POINTS:
    - All collectible values are doubled for 5 seconds
• Collectibles lose value over time, so move quickly
• Night Mode (N): Toggle visibility
• Restart (R): Restart the game during play
• Leaderboard (L): View high scores
• Pause (P): Pause the game
